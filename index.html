    About Us

    Fitness is about so much more than exercise. It's a catalyst for positive change. It is not about being better than someone else. It’s about being better than you used to be. Nothing will work unless you do. What hurts today makes you stronger tomorrow. If it doesn’t challenge you, it won’t change you. To keep the body in good health is a duty, otherwise we shall not be able to keep our mind strong and clear.

    When you focus on your health, you awaken your creativity. By creativity I don’t mean painting or drawing, the ability to conceive your life exactly the way you most want it. CRIDAD FITNESS we help you to achieve these goals.
